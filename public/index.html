Who I Am

Hello! I'm Abubakar, a Data Science student at IIT Madras and an aspiring Software Engineer based in Khammam, Telangana. My interest in technology began at the age of 13 during the COVID-19 lockdown, when I wrote my first program—"Hello World" in C. What started as curiosity quickly developed into a passion for programming, leading me to build simple applications such as calculators and software tools for local businesses. Since then, I have been continuously expanding my knowledge in software engineering, data science, web development, and modern technologies. I enjoy solving real-world problems through technology and am committed to developing innovative, scalable, and impactful software solutions.
